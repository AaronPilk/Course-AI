# NextGen modernization

## What NextGen is

FAA's multi-decade upgrade to ATC. Started early 2000s. Components:
- ADS-B replacing radar.
- GPS replacing VOR.
- Data Comm (text) supplementing voice.
- Performance-Based Navigation.
- System-Wide Information Management (SWIM).

For: where ATC is going.

## ADS-B

- Aircraft broadcasts GPS position.
- Ground stations + other aircraft receive.
- Required Jan 2020 most controlled airspace.
- ~$5k retrofit for GA aircraft.

For: ADS-B core piece.

## PBN (Performance-Based Navigation)

Routes specified by GPS waypoints, not VOR-to-VOR:
- More direct.
- More precise.
- Better noise + emissions profile.

Standard RNAV procedures.

For: efficient routing.

## OPD (Optimized Profile Descents)

Continuous descents from cruise (vs. step-down):
- Less fuel.
- Less noise.
- Less ATC workload.

Major airports adopting.

For: greener arrivals.

## Data Comm

Text-based ATC instructions:
- Loaded directly into FMS.
- Reduces voice frequency congestion.
- Easier to verify.

Deployed at major airports.

For: voice replacement.

## SWIM

System-Wide Information Management:
- ATC, airlines, airports share data.
- Real-time weather, schedules, capacity.
- Industry interoperability.

For: collaboration infrastructure.

## RNP (Required Navigation Performance)

Some routes require RNP authorization:
- High accuracy GPS.
- Aircraft monitors own performance.
- Enables tight curves through mountains (Alaska, Juneau).

For: specialized routing.

## Wake Turbulence Recategorization

New separation standards based on aircraft type, not just weight class:
- Closer-in same-class.
- More efficient runway use.

Increases airport capacity.

For: airport optimization.

## Trajectory-Based Operations (TBO)

Future state:
- Aircraft trajectory pre-negotiated.
- ATC tracks deviations.
- Reduces tactical intervention.

In development.

For: futuristic ATC.

## Equivalent investments

European SESAR (Single European Sky ATM Research):
- Similar goals.
- Cross-EU coordination.
- Joint programs with FAA.

For: international.

## Capacity vs. demand

Pre-pandemic US airspace ~70k commercial flights/day. Capacity ceiling without NextGen ~80k.

Without modernization: chronic delays.

For: motivation.

## Costs

NextGen estimated $35-40B (federal + airline investment).

Critics: behind schedule, over budget. Defenders: enabling fundamental capability.

For: realistic context.

## Drones in airspace

UTM (Unmanned Aircraft System Traffic Management):
- Separate from manned aviation.
- Low altitude (<400 ft).
- Remote ID for tracking.
- Beyond-visual-line-of-sight (BVLOS) operations emerging.

For: airspace expansion.

## Mistakes to avoid

- **Expecting overnight changes.** Decades-long program.
- **Ignoring training.** Pilots + controllers must adapt.
- **Underestimating costs.** Major capital expense.

## Summary

- NextGen: GPS + ADS-B + Data Comm + PBN.
- ADS-B replacing radar (2020+ mandate).
- PBN replacing VOR navigation.
- OPDs greener arrivals.
- UTM for drone integration.
- 35-40B investment; decades-long program.
