# eVTOL + urban air mobility

## What eVTOL is

Electric Vertical Takeoff and Landing aircraft.

Key features:
- Multiple electric rotors.
- Vertical takeoff (no runway).
- Quiet vs. helicopters.
- Battery-powered.
- Designed for urban air mobility (UAM).

For: flying car category.

## Major players

- **Joby Aviation.** Toyota-backed; FAA cert in progress.
- **Archer Aviation.** Stellantis partnership.
- **Beta Technologies.** Cargo + passenger.
- **Lilium.** German jet-design.
- **EHang.** Chinese pioneer; autonomous focus.

For: industry landscape.

## Use cases

- **Vertiport-to-vertiport.** Manhattan to JFK in 7 min.
- **Air taxi.** Skip traffic.
- **Cargo / medical.** Time-critical deliveries.
- **Emergency services.** Faster response.

For: applications.

## FAA certification

Joby et al. pursuing FAA Type Certificate:
- Part 23 (small aircraft) framework adapted.
- New requirements for electric + vertical flight.
- 2-3 years typical certification.

First commercial flights: 2025-2026.

For: regulatory path.

## Range + payload

Current state-of-art:
- ~100-150 mile range.
- 4-6 passengers.
- ~150 mph cruise.

Battery improvements key to expansion.

For: realistic limits.

## Infrastructure

Vertiports:
- Urban locations (rooftops, transit hubs).
- Multiple landing pads.
- Charging stations.
- Air traffic flow.

Costs ~$10-50M each.

For: deployment cost.

## Air traffic for UAM

Existing airspace insufficient. Solutions:
- New UAM corridors.
- Lower altitude (200-500 ft).
- Dedicated routes.
- AI-managed traffic.

Active FAA + NASA research.

For: airspace evolution.

## Pilots vs. autonomous

Early eVTOLs: piloted (1 pilot for safety + cert).
Future: autonomous.

Tech ready for limited autonomous; regulation lagging.

For: career evolution.

## Eviation Alice + electric commuters

Eviation, Heart Aerospace pursuing:
- 9-passenger electric commuters.
- 200-400 mile range.
- Regional aviation electrification.

Different category from eVTOL.

For: adjacent tech.

## Hybrid options

Hybrid-electric:
- Bridge to full electric.
- Extended range via gas backup.
- Lower emissions than ICE only.

ATR + others developing.

For: transition tech.

## Hydrogen fuel cell aircraft

Future:
- Range advantages.
- Zero emissions (water vapor only).
- ZeroAvia retrofitting regional aircraft.

For: alternative fuel.

## Sound

Major eVTOL advantage: 65 dB at 100 ft vs. helicopter 95 dB.

Enables urban deployment without massive noise complaints.

For: community acceptance.

## Market projections

eVTOL market 2030: $30-50B projected.

But: many startups; significant consolidation expected.

For: business reality.

## Mistakes to avoid

- **Treating as helicopters.** Different operations.
- **Overestimating timeline.** Cert challenging.
- **Underestimating infrastructure.** Vertiports critical.

## Summary

- eVTOL = electric vertical takeoff aircraft.
- Joby, Archer, Beta leading.
- Urban air mobility use case.
- 100-150 mile range, 4-6 passenger current.
- FAA certification in progress.
- Vertiports + air traffic infrastructure needed.

Next: careers.
