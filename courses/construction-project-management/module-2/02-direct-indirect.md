# Direct + indirect costs

## Direct costs

Tied to specific work:
- **Labor.** Workers placing concrete, framing, etc.
- **Materials.** Lumber, concrete, fixtures.
- **Equipment.** Excavator hours, crane.
- **Subcontracts.** Electrical, mechanical, etc.

Allocatable to specific line item.

For: physical work cost.

## Indirect (general conditions)

Project-wide:
- Project manager + superintendent salaries.
- Site office trailer.
- Portable toilets.
- Job site dumpsters.
- Temporary utilities (power, water).
- Security.
- Cell phones.
- Travel.

Typically 6-15% of direct cost.

For: project overhead.

## Home office overhead

Company-wide:
- Owner's salary.
- Office rent.
- Accounting + admin.
- IT.
- Marketing.
- Insurance (company-wide).

Typically 5-10% of revenue.

For: business overhead.

## Bonds

- **Bid bond.** Guarantees contractor will sign if awarded (~1% project value).
- **Performance bond.** Guarantees contractor will complete (typically 50-100% project value).
- **Payment bond.** Guarantees subs + suppliers paid.

Cost ~1-3% of bonded amount.

For: public + large projects.

## Insurance

- **General liability.** $1M+ minimum typical.
- **Workers compensation.** State-mandated.
- **Builders risk.** Insures building during construction.
- **Auto.** Vehicles.
- **Equipment.** Owned/rented equipment.
- **Umbrella.** Higher coverage above primary.

5-10% of revenue typical.

For: risk transfer.

## Permits + fees

- Building permit (~0.5-3% of project value).
- Impact fees.
- Inspection fees.
- Plan check fees.
- Sometimes: tap fees (utilities).

Often forgotten in early estimates.

For: regulatory cost.

## Taxes

- Sales tax on materials (varies by state).
- Use tax on out-of-state purchases.
- Payroll taxes.

Add to material cost in jurisdictions.

For: tax accounting.

## Equipment cost categories

- **Rental.** Daily/weekly/monthly.
- **Owned with operator.** Internal hourly rate.
- **Owned without operator.** Lower rate.

Choice depends on duration of need.

For: equipment decisions.

## Allowances

In contract:
- Owner allows $X for specific item not yet selected.
- If actual cost > allowance: change order.
- If actual cost < allowance: credit.

Common for: appliances, fixtures, flooring.

For: handling undecided scope.

## Mistakes to avoid

- **No general conditions in estimate.** Underestimated.
- **Wrong tax jurisdiction.** Late-discovered costs.
- **No bond cost.** Project not bondable.

## Summary

- Direct: labor + materials + equipment + subs (per line).
- Indirect (general conditions): PM, site office, utilities (project-wide).
- Home office overhead: company-wide.
- Bonds + insurance + permits + taxes.
- Allowances for undecided items.
