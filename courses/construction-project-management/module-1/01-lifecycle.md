# Bid to closeout phases

## Project phases

1. **Pre-design / Programming.** Define need; budget; site selection.
2. **Schematic Design.** Architect develops concept.
3. **Design Development.** Refinement; engineering.
4. **Construction Documents.** Drawings + specs final.
5. **Bidding / Procurement.** Contractors bid.
6. **Construction.** The work.
7. **Closeout.** Inspections, punch list, final payment.

For: end-to-end view.

## Pre-construction

Critical phase:
- Constructability review.
- Budget estimates.
- Schedule baseline.
- Permits initiated.
- Subcontractor list.

Investing here saves 10× cost later.

For: front-loading wins.

## Construction phases

1. **Site preparation.** Demo, grading.
2. **Foundation.** Excavation, footings, slab.
3. **Vertical structure.** Framing, steel.
4. **Envelope.** Roof, walls, windows.
5. **MEP rough-in.** Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing.
6. **Interior.** Drywall, paint, flooring, finishes.
7. **Commissioning.** Test all systems.

For: sequence understanding.

## Critical milestones

Key gates:
- Notice to Proceed.
- Foundation pour.
- Topping out.
- Substantial completion.
- Certificate of Occupancy.
- Final acceptance.

Each triggers contractual events.

For: contract triggers.

## Substantial completion

"Owner can use building for intended purpose":
- Triggers warranty period.
- Stops liquidated damages.
- Releases retention.

Critical legal milestone.

For: financial trigger.

## Closeout

Final activities:
- Punch list completion.
- As-built drawings.
- Operations + maintenance manuals.
- Warranties.
- Lien releases.
- Final payment.

Often takes 60-90 days.

For: completion process.

## Timeline examples

- Small renovation: 2-6 months.
- Single-family home: 4-12 months.
- Mid-rise office: 12-24 months.
- Hospital: 3-5 years.
- Major airport: 5-10+ years.

For: scale.

## Common delays

- **Permits.** Bureaucracy.
- **Weather.** Unforeseeable.
- **Supply chain.** Materials lead times.
- **Labor shortages.** Skilled trades.
- **Design changes.** Owner-driven.
- **Inspections.** Failing first try.

Schedule buffer essential.

For: risk awareness.

## Mistakes to avoid

- **Rushing pre-construction.** Compounds problems.
- **No clear closeout checklist.** Final payment delayed months.
- **Missing milestones.** Contract penalties.

## Summary

- 7 phases: Programming → Design → Bidding → Construction → Closeout.
- Pre-construction investment saves money.
- Substantial completion = legal milestone.
- Punch list + as-builts close out project.
- Delays common; buffer accordingly.
