# Robot platforms

## Educational platforms

- **TurtleBot 4.** ROS 2 native; ~$2k. Standard learning platform.
- **Crazyflie 2.1.** Tiny drone; ~$200. Easy programming.
- **iRobot Create 3.** Vacuum base for custom builds.

For: hands-on learning.

## Industrial arms

- **UR5e / UR10e (Universal Robots).** ~$30-50k. Collaborative.
- **Franka Emika Panda.** $30k. Research darling.
- **KUKA LBR iiwa.** $50k+. Heavy industrial.
- **Doosan, Kassow.** Newer cobots.

For: arm manipulation.

## Mobile platforms

- **Boston Dynamics Spot.** $75k+. Quadruped.
- **Unitree Go1 / Go2.** $2k-3k. Quadruped affordable.
- **MIR / Otto.** AMRs for warehouses.

For: legged + wheeled mobility.

## Humanoids (2024-2026)

- **Tesla Optimus.** Production starting.
- **Figure 01 / 02.** Warehouse partnerships (BMW).
- **Boston Dynamics Atlas.** Research/demo.
- **Unitree H1.** ~$90k.
- **Agility Robotics Digit.** Logistics.

Booming sector.

For: emerging area.

## Drones

- **DJI Mavic / Phantom.** Consumer/prosumer.
- **Skydio.** Autonomous AI drones.
- **Custom: Pixhawk + ROS 2.** Research builds.
- **Parrot Anafi.** Mid-range.

For: aerial robots.

## Surgical robots

- **Da Vinci.** Intuitive Surgical; gold standard.
- **CMR Surgical Versius.** Modular alternative.
- **Stryker Mako.** Orthopedic specific.

For: medical (regulated; specialized).

## Self-driving

- **Waymo.** Commercial taxis.
- **Cruise.** GM-owned (paused).
- **Tesla FSD.** Camera-only neural net.
- **Mobileye.** ADAS supplier.

For: autonomous vehicles.

## Choosing a platform

Beginner:
- TurtleBot 4 for mobile.
- Niryo or Dobot for arms ($1-5k).

Research:
- Franka Panda or UR5e for arms.
- Spot for legged.

Production:
- Industry-specific.

For: matching to needs.

## Buy vs. build

Buy:
- Faster.
- Tested.
- Spare parts.

Build:
- Cheaper.
- Custom.
- Learn deeply.

For: tradeoff.

## Mistakes to avoid

- **Toy hardware for serious work.** Frustration.
- **Custom build for time-pressured project.** Overrun.
- **Cheap drones for autonomy.** Sensors inadequate.

## Summary

- Educational: TurtleBot, Crazyflie.
- Industrial: UR, Franka, KUKA.
- Mobile: Spot, Unitree, MIR.
- Humanoids: Optimus, Figure, Atlas.
- Match platform to budget + experience + goal.
